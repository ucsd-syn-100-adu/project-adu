What is an ADU?

An Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) is a small, self-contained home on the same lot as a main house. California has made it easier to build ADUs by streamlining approvals and reducing restrictions. They provide rental income, boost property value, and help address the housing shortage.

Our Mission at EcoBuild Connect?

At EcoBuild Connect, we are committed to revolutionizing the way people design and build sustainable Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs). Our mission is to empower homeowners with a fully customizable, eco-friendly ADU solution that seamlessly blends innovation, sustainability, and functionality. Through our intuitive online platform, we connect users with environmentally responsible materials and trusted green construction partners, making sustainable living more accessible and efficient.

By prioritizing energy efficiency, waste reduction, and sustainable sourcing, we aim to create ADUs that not only enhance living spaces but also contribute to a healthier planet. Our vision is to redefine modern housing by offering beautiful, affordable, and environmentally conscious ADUs that meet the needs of today’s homeowners while protecting the future of our communities.
